# 🛠️ Simple Troubleshooting Guide

Having trouble? Here are the 3 most common issues and how to fix them in 1 click!

---

## Issue 1: Printer not found (Receipt won't print)

### How to fix:
1. **Check USB connection:** Unplug the printer USB cable and plug it back in.
2. **Turn printer off and on:** Flip the power switch off, wait 5 seconds, and flip it back on.
3. **Click Scan Hardware:** Go to the **Printer Setup** tab and click the **Scan Hardware** button to re-scan.

![Scan hardware button example](C:/Users/Geloo/Downloads/pos-system/docs/scan_button.png)

---

## Issue 2: App won't start (Black window closes)

### How to fix:
1. **Run Setup again:** Double-click the **`install.bat`** file to make sure all components are installed.
2. **Wait 1 minute:** Sometimes Windows takes a moment to free up the system ports.
3. **Double-click `start.bat`:** Try launching the launcher again.

![Start bat icon example](C:/Users/Geloo/Downloads/pos-system/docs/start_bat.png)

---

## Issue 3: No internet connection (Offline badge)

### How to fix:
- **Don't worry!** The POS works **fully offline**. You can check out items, compute change, and log sales as usual.
- Once your internet is back, the green **ONLINE** badge will reappear and auto-sync your data.

![Offline badge example](C:/Users/Geloo/Downloads/pos-system/docs/offline_badge.png)
