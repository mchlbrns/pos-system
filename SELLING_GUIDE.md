# Value Proposition Selling Guide

Use this guide when pitching the POS system to local business owners in the Philippines.

## 1. Water Station Refilling Shops
- **Pain Point**: Container tracking. Station owners lose hundreds of pesos daily when customers take gallon container refills without returning the containers.
- **Pitch**: The system detects refill items in the cart, matches them against returned container logs, and automatically appends a Gallon Deposit Fee to the bill if containers are missing.

## 2. Laundry Shops
- **Pain Point**: Charge accuracy. Laundromats struggle to bill correctly for loads that vary by decimal weights (e.g. 5.4 kg vs 8.0 kg).
- **Pitch**: Cashier interface allows decimal weight entries. Integrates wash/dry service workflows so items can be tracked as "washing", "folding", or "ready for pick-up".

## 3. Motorcycle Repair Shops
- **Pain Point**: Job orders & Mechanic accountability. Shop owners lose track of spare parts used vs labor fees allocated to different mechanics.
- **Pitch**: Checkout binds labor costs to mechanic names, tracks serialized spare parts from inventory, and prints Job Orders for service technicians.
